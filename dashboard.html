Methodology

How the engine ranks

A transparent, four-stage pipeline from job description to final ranking.

Job Description Analysis

The job posting is parsed and embedded to capture intent, required skills and context.

Semantic Matching · 70%

Each candidate profile is embedded with sentence-transformers and compared by meaning — not keyword overlap.

Behavioural Signals · 30%

Profile completeness, response time and engagement metrics are scored as an activity signal.

Final Ranking

The two signals blend into one fit score. The top 100 of 100,000 candidates are shown here.
